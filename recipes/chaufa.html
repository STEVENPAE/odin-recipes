How to make Arroz Chaufa

Description

Arroz Chaufa is Peruvian Fried RIce made with rice, chicken, hot dogs, bell pepers, and onion in less than 20 minutes!

Ingredients

  • pre-cooked rice
  • hot dogs (optional)
  • chicken
  • green onion
  • bell pepper
  • eggs
  • garlic
  • ginger root
  • soy sauce
  • sesame oil

Steps

  1. Cook hot dogs and veggies.
  2. Scramble the eggs
  3. Add rice and sauce
  4. Mix together and Enjoy!
